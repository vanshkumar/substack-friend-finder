# Privacy Policy for Substack Friend Finder

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Overview

Substack Friend Finder is a browser extension that helps you discover people with similar reading interests based on your Substack newsletter subscriptions. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

### What We Access
- Your Substack username (entered by you)
- Your public Substack profile and subscriptions
- Subscriber and follower lists of newsletters you're subscribed to

### What We Store
All data is stored **locally in your browser** using Chrome's storage API:
- Your scan results (matched profiles)
- Scan progress (so you can resume if interrupted)

### What We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT send any data to external servers
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking
- We do NOT collect personal information
- We do NOT store your Substack credentials
- We do NOT access your email or payment information

## Data Sharing

**We do not share any data with third parties.** All data remains on your device.

## Data Storage

- All data is stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`
- Data persists until you clear your browser data or uninstall the extension
- You can clear stored data by uninstalling and reinstalling the extension

## Permissions

The extension requires these permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `storage` | Save scan progress and results locally |
| `activeTab` | Communicate with the Substack tab |
| `Host permissions (substack.com)` | Access Substack's public APIs |

## Third-Party Services

This extension interacts only with Substack's website and APIs. We do not integrate with any other third-party services.

## Open Source

This extension is open source. You can review the complete source code at:
https://github.com/vanshkumar/substack-friend-finder

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above.
